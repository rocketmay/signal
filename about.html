ABOUT THIS SITE

Trigger Signal is a personal archive of coaching notes and technical reference materials for 10m air gun competitors. The site intentionally reads like a field manual: dense, opinionated, and focused on utility.

EST 2026

Origins

These pages are a collection of personal notes and assembled knowledge around the sport of 10m air pistol. The sport is both extremely technical and also extremely individual. On the technical side, there is a lot of literature out there and a lot of knowledge held by top athletes and coaches for an athlete to learn from. But for every rule of thumb or common wisdom around performance in this sport, there is a high level athlete that breaks those rules and still succeeds.

I am not particularly special in the world of the 10m air pistol, so it’s not that my thoughts should hold any weight, particularly over more accomplished and experienced athletes. Still, I find that hearing the perspective of other athletes is very valuable to me, and know many athletes myself who are hungry for any knowledge. Consider this site yet another resource to digest. Take away the pieces of it that you resonate with, and discard the rest.
